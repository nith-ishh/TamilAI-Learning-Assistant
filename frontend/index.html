Frequently asked questions

Is TamilAI free for students? ↓
Yes! TamilAI is completely free for all school students. Basic features including AI Tutor, Quiz Generator, and Notes are all free. We believe quality education should be accessible to every Tamil student.
Does it support all Samacheer Kalvi subjects? ↓
Yes, TamilAI supports all major subjects from Class 6 to 12 — Science, Mathematics, Social Science, Tamil, and English. We use the official Samacheer Kalvi syllabus as our knowledge base.
Can I upload my own textbook PDFs? ↓
Absolutely. The Textbook Chat feature lets you upload any PDF — official textbooks, private notes, or custom study material. Our AI indexes it using RAG technology so you get answers from within your own content.
How accurate is the Tamil speech recognition? ↓
We use OpenAI's Whisper model fine-tuned for Tamil, achieving over 92% accuracy for school-level Tamil speech. It handles various Tamil dialects including Madurai, Chennai, and Coimbatore Tamil naturally.
Is my data private and secure? ↓
Yes. All student data, chat history, and uploaded documents are encrypted and stored securely. We never share personal data with third parties. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
